Coffee Distribution Dashboard

According to the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO), coffee is one of the most widely consumed beverages globally and is also one of the most traded commodities. The largest coffee-producing countries include Brazil, Vietnam, and Colombia. On the other hand, the European Union and the United States of America are the largest consuming and importing markets for coffee worldwide. If you are interested in further analysis, you can explore a dashboard that covers data from 2000 to 2023, focusing on principal coffee-producing countries.

Coffee Dashboard

Click the dropdown menu above to view the country with the highest values about distribution, production, importation, etc of coffee.

The bar chart gives information about the avarage of porduction between 2000-2023 by country the avarage of porduction between 2000-2023.

The box plot visualizes the distribution of coffee by country, highlighting countries with the highest values in categories like imports, exports, and stocks. To focus on specific countries, use the 'All' option in the drop-down menu and deselect the undesired countries by clicking on their colors. To focus on one country , simply choose its specific name in the drop-down menu.
