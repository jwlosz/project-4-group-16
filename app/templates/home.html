Diabetes is defined as : a chronic, metabolic disease characterized by elevated levels of blood glucose (or blood sugar), which leads over time to serious damage to the heart, blood vessels, eyes, kidneys and nerves.

Stroke is defined as :blood flow to the brain is blocked or there is sudden bleeding in the brain.

Hypertension is defined as : is when the pressure in your blood vessels is too high (140/90 mmHg or higher). It is common but can be serious if not treated.
